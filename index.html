Design should be simple

I don’t have a background in design. This may sound alarming, but I consider a generalist approach an important asset.

I studied Management and Finance at UTS, Sydney, before working as an Ops Manager in high-growth startups. This involved learning fast and wearing a lot of hats. In mid-2019, I decided I wanted to pursue design.

Product design isn't as complicated or as difficult as everyone pretends it is. I believe good design should be simple and clear with a pragmatic focus on business growth.
